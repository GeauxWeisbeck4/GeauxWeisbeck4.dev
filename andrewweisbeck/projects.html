Martin Luther King - Strength to Love

Inspiration & Education

I chose to make a responsive website on the collection of sermons by Dr. Martin Luther King Jr that are organized into a book titled, "Strength to Love", featuring a foreward by Coretta Scott King. This book is an important framework in how I assess and choose my values to live by. I blog about sections of the book and about how it applies to society today and my own life.
